Terra Liquidity Alliance

The Alliance Liquidity Hub converts Terra staking rewards into liquidity incentives. Lock LUNA to vote on where liquidity rewards go, earn rebases and voting incentives, and help build protocol-owned liquidity for the Terra ecosystem.

How It Works:

Voters: Lock LUNA/ampLUNA/bLUNA for voting power (up to 10x with 2-year lock)

Vote: Decide how liquidity rewards are distributed to LP assets via gauge voting

Earn: Receive rebases based on locked LUNA + voting incentives from projects

LPs: Provide liquidity and receive rewards based on voting results
