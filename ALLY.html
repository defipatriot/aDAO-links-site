Where Do The Rewards Come From?

The reward mechanism is powered by Terra's innovative Alliance module. The same smart contract that minted the NFTs also created a unique asset called "Ally," which is staked to earn a share of the network's staking rewards. This creates a direct link between the health of the Terra blockchain and the value accruing to NFT holders.

On February 23, 2024, the Terra community passed governance proposal #4801 to officially add "Ally" as an Alliance asset. The delegation of the asset is controlled by AllianceDAO governance and can be changed at any time; you can see the Alliance ALLY validator delegations HERE. Staking rewards are collected periodically using Warp Protocol.

The Eris Upgrade: Supercharging Rewards

In March 2024, AllianceDAO voted to partner with Eris Protocol to convert the LUNA rewards into ampLUNA. This upgrade, which went live on June 15, 2024, increases rewards by 40% over four years. In addition, the DAO will receive 5 Million Eris tokens, vested over four years. You can view the complete security audit here.

The Last NFT Standing: A Game of Scarcity

The same amount of rewards flow into the contract regardless of how many NFTs are eligible to claim them. Each "unbroken" NFT receives an equal proportion of the ever-growing reward pool. As users "break" their NFTs to claim rewards, the number of outstanding, unbroken NFTs gets smaller, and the share of the reward pool for those that remain gets larger.

At The Start

If you own one of 10,000 "unbroken" NFTs, your NFT represents a 1/10,000 share of the pool.

As NFTs are Broken

If 6,000 NFTs are broken, your share of the pool grows to become 1/4,000 of the pool.

The longer you hold your NFT without breaking it, the greater your reward will be.

How to Claim Your Rewards

At any point, NFT holders can connect their wallet and use BoostDAO.io Ignite to "break" their NFT to claim their accrued ampLUNA rewards. Importantly, rewards can only be claimed once per NFT. Once an NFT has been broken, no more Alliance staking rewards will accrue to it, but it can still be staked on DAODAO.zone to participate in governance.

The DAO's Vision: Repaying Terra & Forging Alliances

The AllianceDAO is extremely fortunate to receive these rewards directly from the chain. While we have a treasury and other revenue streams, the DAO's current focus is on finding ways to repay the Terra ecosystem for this opportunity. The ultimate goal is to form new alliances with other chains as Terra continues to grow, using this unique NFT collection as a tool to forge those powerful new partnerships and bring value back to the entire ecosystem.

AllianceDAO NFTs are transferable. If you send an unbroken NFT to someone, the wallet in possession of the NFT will be the only user that can claim the rewards.
