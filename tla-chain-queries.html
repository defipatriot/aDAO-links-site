Chain Query Builder

Pre-built smart-contract queries against the Eris ve3 stack on Terra. Pick a query, fill in any required arguments, and get a one-click chainscope.io link or the raw JSON. Contract addresses auto-resolve from the live catalog.

How this works

Every query is documented with what it does and what shape of response to expect. no args queries are one click away — no setup needed. needs args queries let you pick from known wallets in the catalog or paste any address. All queries open in chainscope.io.
