The model: for standard (xyk) pools, price impact = trade ÷ (the sold token's pool reserve + trade) — the exact constant-product execution-price formula on live reserves. Concentrated and stable pools bunch liquidity near the current price, so their true impact is lower than shown — those rows carry "≤" and grade on the bound. This ignores swap fees, multi-hop routing, and same-block competition: it's a planning number, not a quote. Grades: A<0.5% · B<1% · C<3% · D<8% · F above.
