How Rewards are Generated

The AllianceDAO NFT contract holds a unique token called "Ally." Through a community-passed governance proposal, this token was added as a Terra Alliance asset, allowing it to earn a percentage of all LUNA staking rewards generated by the network. This creates a powerful new reward stream, fundamentally different from traditional staking.

How to Claim Your Rewards

At any time, you can "break" your NFT to claim your share of the ampLUNA rewards. This is a one-time action; once an NFT is broken, it no longer accrues rewards but can still be used for governance. Unbroken NFTs are fully transferable, and the rewards travel with the NFT to the new owner.

The DAO's Vision: Repaying the Favor

The Alliance DAO is incredibly fortunate to be one of the first projects to receive rewards directly from on-chain inflation. While we have other revenue streams, our core focus is on finding innovative ways to repay the Terra ecosystem for this opportunity. Our ultimate goal is to leverage our unique position to forge new alliances with other chains as Terra grows, using our collection as a bridge to build a stronger, more interconnected future.

Important Disclaimer: The on-chain rewards allocated to the 'Ally' token are a privilege granted by the Terra community via governance. These rewards are not guaranteed and can be modified or removed at any time by a future governance proposal. All projections and historical data are based on past performance and are not indicative of future results. Daily Gain data is based on a snapshot.
